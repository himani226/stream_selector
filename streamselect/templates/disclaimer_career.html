Career Selection Test


The Hand Holding and Complete Career Guidance program for +2 pass students involves several key steps:

1. Stream selection and state quota eligibility criteria:

Students will be informed about the different stream options available and the eligibility criteria for admission into professional colleges in various states, including the number of seats available and the benefits of studying in a particular state.

2. Study pattern and competitive exam preparation:

During the two years leading up to the final +2 examination, students will receive tips on how to study effectively, how to prepare for competitive exams, and which exams they are eligible to take.

3. Career options after +2:

After the final exams and competitive exams, students will be informed about other career options available to them.

4. Online PTM:

The program will include online parent-teacher meetings every six months to provide updates on the student's progress and offer any additional support or guidance as needed.

This program aims to provide comprehensive support and guidance to students as they navigate the challenging and often confusing transition from high school to higher education and beyond.
